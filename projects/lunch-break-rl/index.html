About

Lunch Break RL is a multiplayer roguelike playable through Twitch chat.

This project was an experiment to build a project a single hour at a time over my lunch break. I typically spent about 45 minutes doing development work and ended the stream with 15 minutes of gameplay. The project evolved into a multiplayer Twitch Roguelike where the viewers directly controlled characters in a downward battle through more and more difficult dungeon levels.
